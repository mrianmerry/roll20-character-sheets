# Mekton Zeta (Advanced)

I decided to create this sheet to enable playing Mekton Zeta with a more functional-oriented character sheet. I wanted to enable players to plug in things like the properties of their EMW, and then have the sheet worry about ensuring the right values are calculated.

As such, there's probably still going to be use-case for the [other Mekton sheet](https://github.com/Roll20/roll20-character-sheets/tree/master/Mekton%20Z); it's much more homebrew friendly than this will be. That said, feel free to reach out to me and let me know of any feedback and suggestions, either on [my character sheet fork](https://github.com/mrianmerry/roll20-character-sheets/issues) or directly to [my Roll20 account](https://app.roll20.net/users/1319761/ian).
